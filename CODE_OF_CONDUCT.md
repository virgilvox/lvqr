# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Be respectful, constructive, and professional. We are building open source software together.

Welcome newcomers. Answer questions patiently. Assume good intent.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discrimination of any kind
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to hackbuildvideo@gmail.com. Maintainers will review and take appropriate action.
